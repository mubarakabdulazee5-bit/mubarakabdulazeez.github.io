Your Eyes, Your Vision, Your Health

Learn about common eye conditions, how to prevent them, and protect your vision for life.

Common Eye Conditions

Cataract

Cataracts cause cloudy vision due to protein build-up in the lens. Prevent with UV protection, nutrition, and regular eye exams.

Glaucoma

Glaucoma damages the optic nerve from high pressure inside the eye. Early detection is vital to prevent permanent blindness.

Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

AMD affects central vision. Maintain a healthy diet rich in vitamins C and E, and avoid smoking to reduce risk.

Diabetic Retinopathy

Caused by damaged retina blood vessels due to diabetes. Control blood sugar and get regular eye check-ups.

Age-Related Eye Problems

Presbyopia

Difficulty focusing on close objects due to aging. Correct with reading glasses or multifocal lenses.

Dry Eyes

Decreased tear production leads to irritation. Use artificial tears and stay hydrated.

Floaters

Small dark shapes drifting in vision. Usually harmless but see a doctor if sudden increase occurs.

Prevention & Eye Care Tips

  • Eat leafy greens, carrots, citrus fruits, and omega-3 rich foods.
  • Wear sunglasses to block harmful UV rays.
  • Limit screen time and follow the 20-20-20 rule.
  • Get regular comprehensive eye exams.
  • Quit smoking and maintain a healthy weight.
